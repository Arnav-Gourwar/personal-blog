Review on the movies

Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

This movie is awesome but yet doesn't live up to Avengers: Infinity War! The violence in here is actually not constant and intense! Yes, there are some moments intense violence but really it is actually tame; meaning: it doesn't keep on going! The ending was sad but yet perfect! I actually expected more sci-fi action/violence but man was I surprise! This had action violence but it wasn't really constant nor was it intense! There is a sad moment where someone dies and it actually made me feel sad inside. The battle in Avengers: Endgame was intense with lots of fighting but one moment it really got intense! I loved the scene where the girl "heroes" fight with each other, showing that girls can fight and will be willing to!
